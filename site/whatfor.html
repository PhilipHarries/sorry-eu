Sorry for...

On the 23rd June 2016, the United Kingdom held a referendum on membership of the European Union. The result of this referendum was to leave the EU.

This has not had what you might call a stabilising effect.

And it's not like it's just about the economy... This is hardly a time for a major European power to be flouncing out because we didn't get our way. Especially since we did get our way, most of the time. It's like the prima donna crying because the chocolates and flowers weren't nice enough.

They were nice enough. I'm sorry we couldn't see that.

Sorry.
